## 三、长版介绍（社交媒体/帖子用 / Long promo version）

### 中文

**大模型能读网页、读文件、读代码——唯独读不了视频。**

录了一小时的会议、收藏了看不懂的外语教程、想快速过完一期长播客……这些对 AI 来说一直是一堵墙。云端的视频理解服务要么按分钟收费，要么得把内容上传到别人的服务器，而且大多只有"听"，没有"看"。

**Let AI Read Video! 把这堵墙拆了。** 它是一个纯本地运行的视频阅读 skill：

- 🎬 **双通道理解**——faster-whisper 在本机 GPU 上做语音转写，ffmpeg 按场景切换 + 均匀采样智能抽帧，"听到的"和"看到的"互相印证
- ⏱️ **全程带时间戳**——AI 的每句回答都能落到 `t=MM:SS`，点回去就是原片位置
- 🔒 **数据不出机**——不调用任何云端视觉/语音 API，隐私视频、内部会议放心喂
- ⚡ **游戏本就够快**——RTX 4060 实测：11 分钟视频全管线 40 秒，1 小时视频 3~5 分钟
- 🧩 **AI 无关**——Kimi、Claude Code、Codex……任何能跑命令行、能看图的 Agent，读一遍 `SKILL.md` 就会用
- 📺 **B站特化**——直接读取本机客户端缓存，免下载、纯离线

一句话：把这个 skill 丢给你的 AI，它就长出了眼睛和耳朵。

### English

**LLMs can read web pages, files, and code — but not videos.**

An hour-long meeting recording, a foreign-language tutorial, a podcast you want to skim — these have always been a wall for AI. Cloud video-understanding services charge by the minute, require uploading your content to someone else's servers, and most of them only "listen" without "seeing".

**Let AI Read Video! tears down that wall.** It's a fully local video-reading skill:

- 🎬 **Dual-channel understanding** — faster-whisper transcribes speech on your local GPU while ffmpeg extracts frames via scene detection + uniform sampling; what it *hears* and what it *sees* cross-validate each other
- ⏱️ **Timestamped everything** — every claim in the AI's answer lands on a `t=MM:SS` citation you can jump back to
- 🔒 **Nothing leaves your machine** — no cloud vision/speech APIs; safe for private videos and internal meetings
- ⚡ **A gaming laptop is enough** — measured on an RTX 4060: an 11-minute video fully processed in 40 seconds; a 1-hour video in 3–5 minutes
- 🧩 **Agent-agnostic** — Kimi, Claude Code, Codex… any agent that can run shell commands and view images just reads `SKILL.md` and gets to work
- 📺 **Bilibili-optimized** — reads the local cache of the official desktop client directly: no download, fully offline

In one line: hand this skill to your AI, and it grows eyes and ears.
